Simon Santschi

About me

I am a PhD student in mathematics at the University of Bern in Switzerland. I started my PhD in August 2022 under the supervision of George Metcalfe. I am mainly interested in algebraic logic and ordered algebraic structures. I am a board member of the SGSLPS (Swiss Graduate Society for Logic and Philosophy of Science).
